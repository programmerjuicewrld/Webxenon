Training

The training offered by AgTraIn varies depending on the size of the company and the needs of the business. It may involve in-house training supervised by a senior and more experienced colleague, or be a mixture of external short courses and project work

We are a group of consultant working on the technical side of agricultural consultancy.

Courses range from one or two days in length to 2 weeks intensive training. As an independent consultant, it's our responsibility to keep up to date both in your specialist area and in the agricultural sector overall. This is done mainly through personal contacts, short courses and attendance at conferences.
